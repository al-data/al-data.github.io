About priority

The game uses this value to define targeting priorities. The enemy with the highest priority value will be chosen as a target. This only applies to attacks and skills that apply such targeting scheme.

Players commonly refer to said targeting as 'targeting elites' or 'targeting humanoids'. However, repair and commander ships in chapter 13 have higher priority than even bosses.
