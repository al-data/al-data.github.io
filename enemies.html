About priority

The game uses this value to define targeting priorities. The enemy with the highest priority value will be chosen as a target. This only applies to attacks and skills that apply such targeting scheme.

Players commonly refer to said targeting as 'targeting elites' or 'targeting humanoids'. However, repair and commander ships in chapter 13 have higher priority than even bosses.

Notes
  • Boss enemies are enemies that must be defeated on the last wave of the boss node. For example, in 9-4 only Niizuki is the boss.
  • Reinforcement enemies are enemies that spawn along with the predefined enemies for a specific wave. Most of them keep respawning, with a time delay, as long as the wave isn't completed and no other reinforcements are alive. Typical reinforcements are suicide boats, some production ships and AA ships.
